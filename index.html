Sustainability

Tourism New Zealand's purpose is to enrich New Zealand by maximising the contribution of domestic and international visitors.

100% Pure New Zealand are members of Tiaki – Care for New Zealand governance group and in collaboration with Tourism Industry Aotearoa and Sustainable Tourism Members.

By supporting the New Zealand Tourism Sustainability Commitment, our aim is to see every New Zealand tourism business committed to sustainability by 2025.
